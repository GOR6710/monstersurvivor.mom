Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while upgrading your character and unlocking new abilities.

With each level up, you'll gain access to powerful new weapons and skills that will help you fend off the increasingly difficult hordes of enemies.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose new abilities and upgrades when leveling up
  • Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters, each with unique starting abilities and playstyles.

Weapon Upgrades

Collect and upgrade various weapons to create the ultimate monster-slaying arsenal.

Endless Gameplay

No two runs are the same with procedurally generated enemy waves and power-ups.

Tips & Strategies

Monster Survivors may seem simple at first, but mastering it requires strategy and quick thinking. Here are some tips to help you survive longer:

  • Focus on movement speed early to avoid getting surrounded
  • Prioritize area damage weapons when facing large groups
  • Don't ignore defensive upgrades - they become crucial in later waves
  • Try to maintain a safe distance from enemies while letting your weapons do the work
  • Experiment with different character and weapon combinations to find your preferred playstyle
